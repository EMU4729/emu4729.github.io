Experimental Mayhem Unit

We are FIRST Team 4729, an Australian robotics team based in UNSW.
EMU competes in FIRST Robotics Competition and FIRST Tech Challenge.
Welcoming high school students of any experience level.

Skill areas

Building a robot requires a wide range of skills areas, all of which you'll have a chance to participate in. You don't need any exprience; our mentors are here to develop your skills.
