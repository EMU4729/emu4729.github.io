Skill areas

Building a robot requires a wide range of skill areas, all of which you'll have a chance to participate in. You don't need any exprience; our mentors are here to develop your skills.

Mechanical

To construct the robots we use tools such as 3D printers and laser cutters. Don't forget the hand tools such as a hack saw or a sand mill.

Computer-aided design

We use a CAD software called Inventor which is used to create the models for the robots. We do this as we need to have a rough idea of what we are building as we only have limited materials.

Electrical

Obviously we need electrical components on the robot so it can actually move. We use things such as the RoboRIO and other electrical components to build the robot.

Software

We have a software team which is dedicated to proggraming the robot. We used to proggram with java but are moving to c++ as, well java is java.

Media

We use media as a way of promoting the team to others, getting other to join and so on. We also connact possible sponsurs, to see if they want to sponsur us.

Who it's for

We're looking for high school students, in Year 8-12 (ages 14+), who are willing to travel to UNSW in Kensington. We meet on Wednesdays and every second Monday from 5 PM to 8 PM in LG09 (lower ground floor) in the Tyree Energy Technologies Building.
