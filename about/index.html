About EMU4729

We are a team of appoximately 20 students who compete in FIRST robotic competitions. We have a group of mentors who help us along the way with building robots.

History of EMU4729

We were created in 2012 and about that time there where only 4 mentors and 8 students.

2016, was our first big year as we won The Judges Award in FRC. We also won the Motivate Award in 2016.

In 2017 we grew from 6 students in 2012 to over 20 students.

In 2018 we one the Gracious Professionalism Award at DDU2018, as we helped other teams.
